Safety and Security

Our Commitment to Safety

At University Marketplace, we prioritize the safety of our users. We are committed to providing a secure environment where students can buy, sell, and connect with confidence. Our platform follows best practices in data protection, secure transactions, and community guidelines to ensure a trustworthy experience for everyone.

Safety Guidelines

  • Verify the seller and buyer before making transactions.
  • Use secure payment methods for all transactions.
  • Report any suspicious activity to our support team immediately.
  • Do not share personal information (like addresses or bank details) without ensuring the security of the transaction.
  • Meet in public, well-lit areas for in-person exchanges.
  • Follow our marketplace rules and respect the community.

Tips for a Safe Experience

To help you make the most of your experience on the marketplace, here are some safety tips:

  • Never share your account details or password with anyone.
  • Trust your instincts. If something feels off, don’t hesitate to contact us.
  • Use our platform’s messaging system to communicate with sellers and buyers.
