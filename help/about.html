About Us

Our Mission

At Marketplace, we strive to connect neighbourhoods in a way that makes sellers and buyers lifes more efficient, affordable, and fun. Our mission is to provide a platform where neighbourhoods can buy, sell, and trade items like textbooks, electronics, clothing, and more. We also support people in launching their own small businesses, side hustles, and creative projects, offering a community-driven space to grow and thrive.

Our Team

We are a group of passionate people, developers, and entrepreneurs who believe in the power of a connected community. Together, we’ve built this platform to serve the needs of people, and we’re always working to improve it based on your feedback!

Our Vision

We envision a neighbourhood where people can easily find what they need, connect with each other, and turn their ideas into reality. We believe in empowering people and providing the tools and support they need to succeed both in and outside their neighbourhoods.
